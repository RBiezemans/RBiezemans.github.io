PhD research

Difficult multiscale problems and non-intrusive methods

During my PhD studies, I am looking into numerical methods of MsFEM type, in particular for multiscale non-self-adjoint problems such as the advection-diffusion equation. Approaches to make these methods as little intrusive as possible are also studied.
